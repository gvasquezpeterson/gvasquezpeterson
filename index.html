Gabriel Vasquez-Peterson

Ph.D. Candidate, Department of Philosophy
University of Pittsburgh

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Department of Philosophy at the University of Somewhere, advised by Jed Lewinsohn. I work on primarily in normative ethics, and my dissertation focuses on partiality and the ethical significance of interpersonal relationships (particularly our intimate relationships). I also have interests in political philosophy, bioethics, metaethics, and the history of philosophy.

Before starting my Ph.D., I completed my BA in Philosophy at the University of Florida in Gainesville.
